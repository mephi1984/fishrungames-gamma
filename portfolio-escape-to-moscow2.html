Escape to Moscow 2

Escape to Moscow 2 is a sequel to Escape To Moscow. It is a visual novel game, available for Android, Windows and Web.

The game takes place in modern Russia, some time after the events of the bad ending of the first part. The main character will try to correct his mistakes and again rushes to meet adventures!

In this game, you will have to help your friend Aliya escape from home again. During the game, you will need to make choices that will affect the plot. An exciting journey awaits you in Moscow and beyond. The goal of the game is to achieve a happy ending, which depends on the choices you make during the game.
