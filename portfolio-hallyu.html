Hallyu Korean Wave App

Hallyu is an application for Android and iOS developed and published in 2015.

It is designed for those who want to study Korean, and it provides following features:

  • Built-in korean word analyzer.
  • Korean lessons.
  • Dictionary.
  • Knowledge tests.
  • Newsfeed with comments.
  • Web comics with translation.
