AR and object detection

Fish Run Games provides services for object tracking, face tracking and applying filter effects.

Since 2017 we work with a variety of CV technologies, which include:

  • OpenCV
  • Caffe2
  • TensorFlow
  • Torch7
  • OpenVino
  • ARKit

Our approach is based on the understanding on how does the CV work "under the hood". That allows us to adjust the technology to the customer's needs, providing better performance than technologies of the general usage.
