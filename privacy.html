Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Fish Run Games ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website fishrungames.com (the "Website"). We are committed to protecting the privacy of our visitors and users. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your personal data in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR).

2. Data Controller Contact Details

For the purposes of the GDPR, Fish Run Games is the Data Controller of your personal data.

  • Company: Vladislav Khorev PE
  • Contact Email for Privacy Matters: contact@fishrungames.com

3. Data We Collect and Legal Basis

We collect and process personal data based on the following legal bases: Consent (for the contact form) and Legitimate Interest (for analytics, to improve our services).

Data Category Purpose of Collection Source of Data Legal Basis (GDPR)
Contact Form Data To respond to your inquiry and communicate with you. Direct submission by user. Consent (By submitting the form)
Usage Data To analyze website traffic, understand user behavior, and improve site performance. Automatic tracking via Google Analytics. Legitimate Interest

3.1. Contact Form Data

When you use our contact form, we collect the following personal data:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Message content

3.2. Usage Data (Google Analytics)

We use Google Analytics to collect information about your visit to our Website. This includes, but is not limited to:

  • IP address (anonymized where possible)
  • Browser type and operating system
  • Pages viewed and session duration
  • Referring website

This data is processed by Google on our behalf. We rely on the Legitimate Interest basis to analyze this data to ensure the technical stability of the website and to measure its performance. You are free to opt-out of Google Analytics tracking using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

4. How Long We Keep Your Data (Data Retention)

We will only retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfil the purposes we collected it for, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

  • Contact Form Data: Typically retained for up to 6 months after your last interaction, unless longer retention is required by law.
  • Google Analytics Data: Data is retained according to the standard retention settings of Google Analytics (e.g., 26 months), which we configure to adhere to GDPR.

5. Your Data Protection Rights (GDPR Rights)

  1. The Right to Access: You have the right to request copies of your personal data we hold.
  2. The Right to Rectification: You have the right to request that we correct any information you believe is inaccurate or incomplete.
  3. The Right to Erasure (Right to be Forgotten): You have the right to request that we erase your personal data, under certain conditions.
  4. The Right to Restrict Processing: You have the right to request that we restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  5. The Right to Object to Processing: You have the right to object to our processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.
  6. The Right to Data Portability: You have the right to request that we transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

6. Requesting Data Deletion or Access

If you wish to exercise any of your rights, including making a request for the erasure (deletion) of your data, please contact us using one of the following methods:

  • By Email: Send your request to contact@fishrungames.com.
  • By Contact Form: Submit your request through the contact form available on our Website, clearly stating that you are making a privacy-related request.

We will respond to all legitimate requests within one month.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We keep our Privacy Policy under regular review and will place any updates on this web page. This Privacy Policy was last updated on November 6, 2025.
